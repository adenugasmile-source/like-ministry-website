Our Calling

Spreading Love Through Kindness

"Where love meets action, transformation begins."

Heart of Compassion

At L.I.K.E. Ministry, we believe that love is not merely spoken—it is demonstrated through tangible acts of kindness. Our mission is rooted in the transformative power of compassion, reaching the underserved communities of Nigeria with practical support and spiritual nourishment.

Global Reach, Local Impact

From the bustling streets of Lagos to the rural villages of Ogun State, our outreach programs bring hope, education, healthcare, and economic empowerment. We partner with local communities to create sustainable change that honors dignity and fosters self-reliance.

Faith in Action

We are more than a ministry—we are a movement. Through discipleship, mentorship, and community fellowship, we equip the next generation of Nigerian leaders to carry the torch of kindness forward, creating a ripple effect of love across the continent and beyond.

In Action

Our Outreach & Fellowship

Witness the hands and feet of our ministry at work—serving, loving, and transforming communities across Nigeria.

Street Outreach

Weekly distributions of food, clothing, and medical supplies to vulnerable communities in Lagos and beyond. Our volunteers bring dignity and hope to those in need.

Home Fellowship

Intimate small groups meeting in modern homes across Nigeria, building deep relationships, studying Scripture, and supporting one another through life's journey.

Professional Network

Connecting modern Nigerian professionals in faith-based business mentorship, career development, and entrepreneurial support—dressed in contemporary and traditional elegance.

A Word From Our Founder

Pastor Tunde Adenuga

Founder & Lead Pastor

"When God called me to ministry, He did not ask me to build a cathedral. He asked me to build people. Every smile we restore, every belly we fill, every soul we reach—that is our sanctuary. L.I.K.E. Ministry exists because love must be tangible, kindness must be visible, and faith must be lived."

For over fifteen years, I have witnessed the transformative power of simple acts of love. From the bustling markets of Lagos to the quiet villages of the countryside, I have seen how a single gesture of kindness can reignite hope in a weary heart.

Our ministry is not defined by walls or titles—it is defined by the lives we touch. We are a family of believers committed to demonstrating the gospel through action, not just words. Whether you join us in Nigeria or partner with us from across the ocean, you are part of this divine mission.

I invite you to walk this journey with us. Together, we will show the world that love, in its purest form, is the most powerful force on earth.

Pastor Tunde Adenuga

Founder, L.I.K.E. Ministry
